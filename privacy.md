We may obtain information about your general internet usage by using a cookie file which is stored on your computer. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computerâ€™s hard drive. They help us to improve our site and to deliver a better and more personalized service. They enable us:

 - To estimate our audience size and usage pattern.
 - To store information about your preferences, and so allow us to customize our site according to your individual interests.
 - To speed up your searches.
 - To recognize you when you return to our site.
 
You may refuse to accept cookies by activating the setting on your browser which allows you to refuse the setting of cookies.
